About ConsultationManager

Streamlining academic consultations between students and faculty members, making it easier to connect, learn, and grow together.

To facilitate seamless academic interactions by providing an efficient and user-friendly platform for scheduling and managing consultations between students and educators.

Our Vision

To become the leading consultation management solution in educational institutions, enhancing the learning experience through organized and accessible academic support.

Key Features

Easy Scheduling

Intuitive booking system for students to schedule consultations with their preferred faculty members or student tutors.

Smart Notifications

Automated reminders and real-time notifications to keep everyone informed about upcoming consultations.

Analytics & Insights

Comprehensive reporting and analytics to track consultation patterns and improve academic support.
